About Me

I am compassionate clinical psychologist with extensive expertise in evidence-based mental health care specializes in treating PTSD, cPTSD, personality disorders, and substance use disorders.

With a PhD from LIU Post and a BA from St. John’s University, My career includes international work with Native American reservations and indigenous communities in India, Vietnam, and Ecuador.

As a Psychologist Level II at NYC Health + Hospitals, I am deeply committed to serving fabulous NYC communities. I hold license LIC# 025539, NPI# 1013671585.

I am based in Forest Hills, NY. Beyond my professional life I'm a writer, a poet, an avid hiker, a loving mom, and a devoted wife, weaving creativity and connection into everything I do. My dog is Gynnie.

My mission is to guide individuals on their journey toward healing and self-discovery, no matter where they come from or what challenges they face.
